Job Posting Select Posting to Apply for: 

Clerk/Typist II

Having gone digital some time ago, there is actually very little clerking or typing needed at LexCorp. We just haven't gotten around to formally changing the name of the position to Dogsbody. This is a part-time job, perfect for retirees, or parents with child-care responsibilities. Sorry, no work from home.

Some heavy lifting involved, up to 22 kilos, or 50 pounds.

Associate Mad Scientist

Associate Mad Scientists work under the direct supervision of Full Mad Scientists, althought they are allowed to develop side-projects on their own. (All side-projects become the solely owned property of LexCorp.) PhDs from accredited universities in Chemistry, Physics, or Engineering preferred. Occassionally, there is a need for Anthropologists and/or Archaeologists for time-travel projects, on a contractual basis. Self-conferred doctorates are not recognized. (Note, we're serious about the "accredited universities", anyone can call him/herself "Doctor So-and-So".)

Henchperson

Henchpersons are the lynchpins of LexCorp. The work is varied, and does require travel. Hours are 72 hours on, 72 hours off. Uniforms, and meals when on duty, provided. Prior henching experience desired, but not required.
